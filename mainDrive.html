Main Drives

Solace & Paws is a compassionate organization committed to improving the lives of animals through several key initiatives:

Feed the Strays

We provide sustenance and care for stray animals, ensuring they receive support.

Stop Unethical Breeding

We campaign against unethical breeding practices, advocating for responsible pet ownership.

Animal Shelter Drive

Offers refuge and care for abandoned animals, providing safety and comfort.

Trap Neuter Release

We implement TNR programs to control stray populations and prevent overbreeding.
